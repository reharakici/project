I took this template from templatemonster and i used bootstrap and jquery libraries also i use image resizer function and function library which i took from internet for make mysql queries easier.

I got Admin Panel for administrate the website.
In this Admin Panel you can add products,sliders,categories and you can see your messages which coming from websites iletisim page.


For Login:
Write your username and password then click Enter or Giriş input.

For Quit:
Click Çıkış from left top side.



Adding Categories:

After login you will see a page and a menu for go pages.
Step 1- Hover Kategoriler on menu
Step 2- Click Kategori Ekle on menu
Step 3- Write categori name on Kategori Adı input.
Step 4- Write your keywords on Etiketler input.
Step 5- Choose a picture for your categorie.
Step 6- Click save.

Adding Products:

After login you will see a page and a menu for go pages.
Step 1- Hover Ürünler on menu
Step 2- Click Ürün Ekle on menu
Step 3- Choose a categorie for your product.
Step 4- Write product name on Ürün Adı input.
Step 5- Write product description on Açıklama input.
Step 6- Write your keywords on Etiketler input.
Step 7- Choose pictures for your product.(Don't have to choose for all inputs.)
Step 8- Click save.

Adding Sliders for Homepage:

After login you will see a page and a menu for go pages.
Step 1- Hover Anasayfa Slider on menu
Step 2- Click Slider Resim Ekle on menu
Step 3- Write slider name on Slider Adı input.
Step 4- Choose your sliders picture.
Step 5- Click save.


How to check messages from website:

Step 1- Click Mesajlar on menu
Step 2- Click any of messages.
(If you want to delete messages click eraser image on message's end line.)


How to look and edit Categories:

Look Categories:
Step 1- Click Kategoriler on menu.

Edit Categories:(You don't have to change everything all of them is optional to change.)
Step 1- Click edit button on your categorie's end line.
Step 2- Write your new categorie name on Kategori Adı input.
Step 3- Choose your new picture for your categorie.
Step 4- Write your new categorie keywords on Etiketler input.
Step 5- Click save.

Delete Categories:(You don't have to change everything all of them is optional to change.)
Step 1- Click eraser button on your categorie's end line.
Step 2- Click okey to your browsers pop up.

Delete Categories:(You don't have to change everything all of them is optional to change.)
